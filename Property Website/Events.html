Golden Visa

The Investor
Program

Why choose the golden visa?

Obtaining Residence Card in 180 days.
Possibility to apply for Portuguese citizenship after five years of residence.
Permission to travel visa-free throughout Schengen EU.
No obligation to reside in Portugal, only one visit of seven days per year.
Wide range of investment options, funds, real estate, culture.

Who can apply?

Real Estate Investment | EUR 500,000. Only in new approved zones, excluding Lisbon, Porto and coastal areas.
Real Estate Investment for Rehabilitation | EUR 350,000. In approved zones
Capital transfer into investment funds or venture capital funds: EUR 500k
Normal capital transfer to Portuguese bank account | EUR 1,500,000
Capital transfer in scientific research institutions | EUR 350,000.
Capital transfer in a commercial company | EUR 350,000..
Artistic production with capital transfer or national equity | EUR 250,000.
Creation of at least ten jobs in Portugal. Processing fees apply.

Family Reunification

Children or stepchildren who are minors or disabled.
Children or stepchildren over the age of 18.
Spouses or partners..
Parents or in-laws.
